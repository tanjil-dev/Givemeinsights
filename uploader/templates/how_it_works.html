How it Works

This web page is designed to make insights easy to access, for everyone.

1

Step 1:

Upload your file—either a Word document or an Excel spreadsheet.
2

Step 2:

Behind the scenes, Python gets to work: it reads your data, analyses it, and generates insights almost instantly, right on your screen.
3

Step 3:

Review the insights. If they're helpful, you can download the files or copy the tables and paste them wherever you need it.

  • Q.Do you store my data?

    No. Your data is processed in real time to generate insights, but it is never stored, retained, or shared. We do not have access to your actual content, and once the analysis is complete, no trace remains. Your privacy is our priority.

  • Q.Is there a limit on the size of the files?

    Yes, there are limits—but they depend on several factors including file size, number of columns etc. Most standard documents work seamlessly. The best way to check is to upload your file and let the system guide you.

  • Q.How long does it take to produce insights?

    In most cases, insights are generated within seconds. Larger or more complex files may take slightly longer, but we aim to keep the experience fast and responsive.

  • Q.What format does the Excel sheet have top be in?

    • Row A of the excel sheet needs to be the column headings
    • Row B onwards of the excel sheet is the data

  • Q.What types of documents can I upload?

    You can upload Word documents, Excel spreadsheets.

  • Q.What kind of insights will I receive?

    Depending on your document, you might receive summaries, trend analysis, keyword extraction, sentiment breakdowns, or visual data representations. The goal is to turn raw content into clear, actionable intelligence.

  • Q.Is this service really free?

    Yes. Give Me Insights is committed to making data-driven understanding accessible to everyone—no subscriptions, no hidden fees, no catch.
